Welcome to the Best RPG Ever Made

Press "Start Game" to begin. Then, click on a character to play as. Two new pictures will appear below the first two; your character is on the left, and your randomly chosen enemy is on the right. Click attack to do physical damage, and special attack to do magical or ranged damage. Keep in mind that sorcerers have stronger special attacking power, while warriors have stronger physical attacking power. Your stats are displayed below the character pictures after the game has started.
